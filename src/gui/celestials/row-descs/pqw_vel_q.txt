The current Q-velocity relative to the parent body.
Q-axis, in the perifocal coordinate system, points perpendicular to the periapsis point in the orbit, on the orbital plane.

    o′.q = mqc
    m = √(|μa|) / r
    q = √(|1 - e^2|)
    c = e < 1: cos E; else: cosh H

...where:
μ = standard gravitational parameter of parent body
a = semi-major axis
r = distance to parent body center
e = eccentricity
E = current (elliptic) eccentric anomaly
H = current hyperbolic eccentric anomaly
