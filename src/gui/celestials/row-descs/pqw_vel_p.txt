The current P-velocity relative to the parent body.
P-axis, in the perifocal coordinate system, points towards the periapsis point in the orbit.

    o'.p = -ms
    m = √|μa| / r
    s = e < 1: sin E; else: sinh H

...where:
μ = standard gravitational parameter of parent body
a = semi-major axis
r = distance to parent body center
e = eccentricity
E = current (elliptic) eccentric anomaly
H = current hyperbolic eccentric anomaly
