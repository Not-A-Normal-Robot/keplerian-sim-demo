The current Y-position relative to the parent body (r.y).

    r.y = e_21 × o.p + e_22 × o.q
    e_21 = cos(ω) sin(Ω) + sin(ω) cos(i) cos(Ω)
    e_22 = cos(ω) cos(i) cos(Ω) - sin(ω) sin(Ω)

...where:
ω = argument of periapsis
Ω = longitude of ascending node
i = inclination
o.p = PQW position's P-component
o.q = PQW position's Q-component
